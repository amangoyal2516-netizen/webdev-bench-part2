How groups work

Three simple steps to find your people

1

Browse by craft or interest

Pick a category that suits your hands today — knitting, watercolour, woodwork. Every group is welcoming to beginners and old hands alike.

2

Join with a single tap

No applications, no waiting list. Press Join and you're in. You can leave any time — your patterns and saved posts stay with you.

3

Share, swap, and gather

Post a project, ask for help on a tricky stitch, or organise a stitch-along. Group leaders host weekly themes and seasonal challenges.
