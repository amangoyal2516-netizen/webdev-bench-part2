Beginner orientation

What to expect

Show length Approximately 2 hours including a 20-minute intermission — you'll be out by around 10:30 PM with time to catch the last train.
2h total
Volume level Comfortable, conversational between songs. Loud during the brass-heavy second set, but never punishing — basic earplugs are stocked at the bar if you want them.
2 / 5
Standing vs seated The floor is standing-only with a few rail spots; the balcony is fully seated. If standing for 2 hours feels long, the balcony tier is the safer pick.
Mixed
Crowd vibe Warm, attentive, slightly older audience that mostly comes for the songs — expect polite applause, swaying, and a little singing along on the encore rather than a mosh.
Gentle
